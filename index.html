Introduction

  • UG Prison Farm Kitalya stands as a symbol of profound rehabilitation and reintegration. Situated in the heart of Uganda, we are not just a correctional facility; we are a place where individuals embark on a transformative journey of self-discovery and growth.

The Roles of UG Prison Farm Kitalya

  • Vocational Education: Equipping inmates with technical skills such as farming, carpentry, and tailoring.
  • Psychological Support: Offering tools for emotional and psychological healing to foster responsibility.
  • Community Engagement: Involving inmates in social responsibility projects to aid reintegration.
  • Spiritual Growth: Providing faith-based programs to encourage reflection and renewal.
