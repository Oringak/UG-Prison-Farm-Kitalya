Vision

A centre of excellence in providing human rights-based correctional services in Africa.

Mission

To contribute to the protection and development of society by providing safe, secure, and humane custody of offenders while placing human rights at the centre of their correctional programmes.

Officer In Charge Statement

The Officer in Charge (OC) is responsible for maintaining order, discipline, and the safe custody of prisoners.

Core Values

  1. Professionalism: We shall adhere to professional codes of conduct.
  2. Reliability: We shall be accountable and trustworthy.
  3. Integrity: We shall uphold ethical values and honesty.
  4. Security: We ensure safety of our clients and property.
  5. Oneness: We exhibit unity of purpose.
  6. Nationalistic: We are nationalistic in outlook.
  7. Discipline: We conform to rules and ethical standards.
